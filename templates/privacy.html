Privacy Policy

Lucy World is built for privacy-first marketers. We minimise the data we collect, store every record securely, and never sell, rent, or trade any personal information.

What we collect

  • Account details: email address and display name so we can authenticate you and deliver product updates.
  • Query telemetry: anonymised keyword searches, language, market, provider counts, and aggregate scoring. We store no IP addresses, user agents, or unique identifiers.
  • AI drafting artefacts: generated outlines, markdown, and published blog posts tied to keyword candidates. These contain no personal data.
  • Support communications: messages you send to our team so we can resolve issues.

How the growth flywheel uses data

The AI blog pipeline operates entirely on aggregated keyword signals. We log the minimum viable metadata—keyword text, language, country, provider counts, and audience score—to decide if a query should advance through the flywheel. Drafts and published posts reuse the stored keyword and high-level performance context only.

  • No personal data or customer content is sent to language models.
  • All prompts redact emails, names, and freeform inputs before processing.
  • Published content lives on Lucy World infrastructure and carries no tracking pixels or cross-site scripts.

Cookies & tracking

We only use strictly necessary cookies for authentication. There are no marketing cookies, third-party analytics scripts, or fingerprinting techniques.

Data residency & retention

  • Primary data centres reside in the EU. Backups are encrypted and stored within the same region.
  • Query telemetry rolls off after 180 days unless it feeds an active growth experiment.
  • Published blog content remains online until manually archived. Removing a post deletes the markdown, HTML, and manifest entries.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your account data at any time.
  • Request a machine-readable export of your projects and query history.
  • Contact us to suppress future processing—your information will be permanently removed from active systems within 30 days.

Contact

Email support@lucy.world for privacy questions, data access requests, or to report an issue.
